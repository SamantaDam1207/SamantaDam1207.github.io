About Me

I am an Electrical Engineering student at the University of Toronto, with a passion for designing and building interactive robots. My technical skills include programming in C++, Verilog, Arduino C++, and more. I have worked on numerous projects, from robot construction to software-defined radios and plant monitoring systems.
